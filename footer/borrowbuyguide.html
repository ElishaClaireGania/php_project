BORROW & BUY GUIDE

How to Borrow a Book

To borrow a book, simply log in to your account, browse the library, and click the 'Borrow' button on the book's page.

How to Buy a Book

Purchasing books is easy! Click 'Buy Now' on the book's page and complete the payment process.

Borrowing Duration & Renewal

Books can be borrowed for up to 7 days. You may renew the book if no other users have requested it.
