Privacy Policy for E-Library

1. Introduction

Welcome to Biblio-Sage. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing access to our digital and physical book collection. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you sign up for an account or make a purchase.
  • Library Activity: Books borrowed, due dates, fines incurred, and purchase history.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website and services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide access to digital and physical books.
  • Process borrowing and purchasing transactions.
  • Send reminders about due dates and overdue books.
  • Apply and process fines for late returns.
  • Improve our e-library services.

4. Borrowing & Purchasing Books

Users may borrow books based on their membership level.

Purchased books will be available for permanent access in digital format or as a physical copy (if applicable).

Borrowed books must be returned on or before the due date to avoid fines.

5. Late Return Fines

If a borrowed book is not returned by the due date, a fine will be charged per day until it is returned.

Failure to return a book for an extended period may result in account suspension.

Fine rates and policies will be displayed in the library portal.

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your personal information.

7. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share data with service providers (such as payment processors) as necessary for transactions.
