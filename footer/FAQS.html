Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

1. What is an E-Library?

E-Library is an online platform that provides access to a variety of books, including educational and fiction genres.


2. Do I need an account to use E-Library?

Yes, you need to create an account to borrow or buy books.

Borrowing & Buying

3. How can I borrow books?

Simply log in, select a book, and click the 'Borrow' button.


4. How long can I borrow a book?

You can borrow a book for up to 14 days. Renewal is possible if the book is not reserved by another user.


5. Can I buy books instead of borrowing?

Yes, you can purchase books by selecting 'Buy Now' and completing the payment process.

Payment & Subscription

6. What are the available payment methods?

We accept credit/debit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and bank transfers.


7. Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're unsatisfied with our service.

Technical Support

8. I forgot my password. What should I do?

You can reset your password by clicking 'Forgot Password' on the login page.


9. The website is not loading properly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact support.


10. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@elibrary.com.
